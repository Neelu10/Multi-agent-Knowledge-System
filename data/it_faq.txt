Q: How to reset password?
A: Go to IT Helpdesk page -> Forgot password.

Q: How to request a new laptop?
A: Raise a ticket in IT Support -> Equipment Request.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Visit the self-service password reset portal or contact IT helpdesk.

Q: How to access the VPN?
A: Use your employee ID and password on the VPN login page. For remote access, enable two-factor authentication.

Q: How to request new hardware?
A: Raise a hardware request ticket in the IT Support portal under 'Assets'.

Q: What should I do if my system is not booting?
A: Try restarting and contact IT support if the issue persists.

Q: How do I install company-approved software?
A: Use the Company App Store or raise a request to IT for installation.

Q: How to set up corporate email on mobile?
A: Configure Outlook with your corporate credentials and enable company security policies.

Q: What is the Wi-Fi password for employees?
A: Employee Wi-Fi credentials are available through the IT portal after login verification.

Q: How can I report a phishing email?
A: Forward the suspicious email to phishing@company.com immediately.

Q: How to get remote desktop access?
A: Submit a VPN + RDP access request through the IT Helpdesk portal.

Q: What to do if I forget my system login password?
A: Contact IT Helpdesk for password reset verification.

Q: How to update antivirus?
A: Antivirus updates are automatic. Ensure your device is connected to the corporate network weekly.

Q: Can I use personal USB drives?
A: No, personal storage devices are restricted for data security reasons.

Q: What is the company’s data backup policy?
A: All official files are backed up automatically to OneDrive and SharePoint daily.

Q: How do I access shared network drives?
A: Access through File Explorer > Network > CompanyShared and use your domain credentials.

Q: What to do if my email storage is full?
A: Archive old emails or request storage extension from IT support.

Q: What browsers are supported for internal applications?
A: Chrome and Edge are the recommended browsers for all internal systems.

Q: How to connect to the printer?
A: Open 'Devices and Printers' → Add Printer → Select your department printer from the list.

Q: How to raise an IT ticket?
A: Visit ithelpdesk.company.com and click ‘Raise Ticket’.

Q: Who to contact for system upgrade approval?
A: Contact your project manager, who will forward the request to IT Procurement.
